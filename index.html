About

Pixel Emotes is a pack of emoticons designed for use with text (of course!)

The pack currently includes 42 different emotes, all hand-pixelled and all really damn cute.

Download

There are currently two different sets of Pixel Emotes - Mono and Chromatic. Pick whichever suits your needs!

Each pack contains the same emoticons, as well as bases with which to make your own.
